Facts :
  • Considered by some to be a "father of the computer".

  • Babbage is credited with inventing the first mechanical computer that eventually led to more complex electronic designs.

  • his famous work is designing a programmable computing device.

  • in 1823 he designed Difference engine, is an automatic mechanical calculator designed to tabulate polynomial functions.

  • During the mid-1830s Babbage developed plans for the Analytical Engine, the forerunner of the modern digital computer.
